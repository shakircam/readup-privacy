Privacy Policy for ReadUp

Last updated: October 12, 2025

1. Information Collection and Use

ReadUp does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No registration or login is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or other identifiers are collected.
  • No background data or analytics are recorded.

2. No Advertising

ReadUp does not display ads or use any advertising SDKs or tracking tools.

3. No Subscriptions or Payments

The app is completely free to use. There are no in-app purchases, paid features, or subscription plans.

4. Local Processing

All app functions (such as reading, word marking, or level selection) occur locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

ReadUp does not use any third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics, or AdMob.

6. Children’s Privacy

ReadUp is suitable for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, no special handling of children's data is required.

7. Changes to This Policy

If future updates require changes to how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated and available within the app and on the Play Store listing.
